Luna, My Cat

Luna is my ESA, or Emotional Support Animal. We adopted her in 2019! She was so tiny when we first got her from the shelter. But she was sassy and loved us dearly.

This is Luna, just over a year later. She has grown quite a bit, and now has a full coat! At first, she didn't like my sister's dog. She still doesn't, but mostly tolerates her now.
